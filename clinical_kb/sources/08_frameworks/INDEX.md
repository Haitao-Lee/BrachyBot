## Key Cross-Society Agreements (Cervix BT)

All major societies agree on:
- **HR-CTV D90 >= 85 Gy EQD2 (alpha/beta=10)**, with 90 Gy aim (GEC-ESTRO, EMBRACE-II, ABS, NCCN, ESGO, ESMO, CSCO)
- **OAR D2cc constraints** (alpha/beta=3): bladder <= 80-85 Gy; rectum <= 65-75 Gy; sigmoid <= 70-75 Gy; bowel <= 70-75 Gy
- **IGABT is the standard of care**, replacing point-A dosing

## Disagreements / Open Questions
- IC/IS applicator routine vs. selective: GEC-ESTRO/EMBRACE-II favor routine for HR-CTV > 4 cm; ABS/NCCN are more permissive
- APBI patient selection: ASTRO "suitable" group is broader than ABS; long-term outcomes of expanded criteria still maturing
- BT for prostate (HDR monotherapy dose/fractionation): ABS 2020 lists 7 different regimens; no single standard
