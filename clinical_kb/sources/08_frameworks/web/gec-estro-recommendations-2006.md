## Dose-Volume Goals (combined EBRT + BT, EQD2)

| Structure | Metric | Goal (α/β=10) | Goal (α/β=3) |
|-----------|--------|---------------|---------------|
| HR-CTV | D90 | ≥ 85 Gy | — |
| IR-CTV | D90 | ≥ 60 Gy | — |
| Bladder | D2cc | — | ≤ 90 Gy |
| Rectum | D2cc | — | ≤ 75 Gy |
| Sigmoid | D2cc | — | ≤ 75 Gy |
| Small bowel | D2cc | — | ≤ 75 Gy |

## Planning Aims
- For small tumors (HR-CTV ≤ 4 cm): HR-CTV D90 ≥ 85 Gy EQD2
- For larger tumors: escalate to 90 Gy EQD2 if OAR constraints permit

## Treatment Plan Evaluation
- DVH-based, not point-based
- Optimization aims to maximize HR-CTV D90 while respecting all OAR D2cc limits
