## Document Type
Chinese national consensus statements and management specifications for brachytherapy.

## Key Documents

### National Health Commission (PRC): 放射性粒子植入治疗技术管理规范 (2009, revised 2017)
- Regulatory framework for ¹²⁵I seed implantation
- Mandates: 3D TPS, post-implant dosimetric verification, CT/MRI guidance
- Quality control: D90 ≥ 90% PD, V100 ≥ 90% target

### Chinese Expert Consensus on Permanent I-125 Seed Implantation (2017)
**Source:** 中华放射肿瘤学杂志 (Chinese Journal of Radiation Oncology) 2017

Indications:
- Localized low- to intermediate-risk solid tumors where EBRT or surgery is suboptimal
- Post-operative or post-RT local recurrence
- Salvage for residual disease

Site-Specific Doses (Chinese consensus, 2017)

| Site | Prescription Dose |
|------|-------------------|
| Prostate (low-risk) | 110–145 Gy (matches ABS/AAPM) |
| Lung (peripheral, ≤ 5 cm) | 80–120 Gy |
| Liver (HCC, metastasis) | 80–120 Gy |
| Pancreas | 80–120 Gy |
| Head and Neck | 80–120 Gy |
| Breast (rare, afterloading) | 34 Gy / 10 fx |

### CSCO Cervical Cancer Guidelines
- Brachytherapy is standard of care for locally advanced disease
- Adopt GEC-ESTRO/ICRU 89 target and OAR definitions
- HR-CTV D90 ≥ 85 Gy EQD2

### CSTRO Brachytherapy Committee Documents
- Recommendations on 3D image-guided BT
- Recommendations on applicator selection
- Training and credentialing standards

### CACA I-125 Seed Implantation Consensus for Pancreatic Cancer (2017)
- Indication: locally advanced unresectable pancreatic cancer
- Prescription dose: 80–120 Gy (matched peripheral dose)
- ¹²⁵I seed activity: 0.4–0.6 mCi/seed
- Combined with EBRT (45–50 Gy) and/or chemotherapy (gemcitabine or FOLFIRINOX)

### Chinese 3D-IGABT for Cervical Cancer (CSTRO 2018, 2023)
- HR-CTV D90 ≥ 85 Gy EQD2
- OAR D2cc per GEC-ESTRO/ICRU 89
- HDR afterloaders standard; ⁶⁰Co and ¹⁹²Ir both used
