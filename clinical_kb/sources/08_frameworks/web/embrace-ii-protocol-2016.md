## Document Type
Prospective interventional cohort study protocol — second-generation EMBRACE.

## Key Differences from EMBRACE-I
- Standardized dose prescription to HR-CTV D90 of **90 Gy EQD2** (α/β=10)
- Mandatory use of IMRT/VMAT for EBRT
- Mandatory simultaneous integrated boost (SIB) to involved nodes
- Mandatory combined intracavitary/interstitial (IC/IS) applicator for bulky HR-CTV (≥ 4 cm)
- Mandatory image-guided adaptive BT planning per GEC-ESTRO/ICRU 89
- Centralized QA including applicator reconstruction, plan review, and protocol compliance

## Treatment Plan

### EBRT
- 45 Gy in 25 fx (± SIB of 55–60 Gy to involved nodes, in 25 fx)
- IMRT/VMAT planning, with image guidance (IGRT, daily CBCT or similar)

### Chemotherapy
- Cisplatin 40 mg/m² weekly, 5–6 cycles

### Brachytherapy
- HDR (or PDR) BT: 4 × 6–9 Gy
- Started before or during the last weeks of EBRT
- Applicator: tandem-ring/ovoid with interstitial needles when HR-CTV width > 4 cm

## Dose Goals (Cumulative EQD2)

| Structure | Goal / Constraint |
|-----------|-------------------|
| HR-CTV D90 | 90 Gy EQD2 (α/β=10) — hard goal |
| IR-CTV D90 | ≥ 60 Gy EQD2 (α/β=10) |
| Bladder D2cc | ≤ 80 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) — hard constraint |
| Rectum D2cc | ≤ 65 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) — hard constraint |
| Sigmoid D2cc | ≤ 65 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) — hard constraint |
| Bowel D2cc | ≤ 65 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) — hard constraint |
| Ureter D2cc | ≤ 75 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) — planning aim |
| Vagina (PIBS/PIBS+2cm) | D2cc ≤ 65–75 Gy |

## Significance
- Currently the most rigorous international protocol for LACC IGABT
- Results inform next-generation ABS/ASTRO/NCCN cervical guidelines
