## Recommended Dose Goals (per ABS)

| Structure | Dose Goal (EQD2, α/β=10) | Dose Constraint (EQD2, α/β=3) |
|-----------|---------------------------|-------------------------------|
| HR-CTV D90 | ≥ 85 Gy | — |
| IR-CTV D90 | ≥ 60 Gy | — |
| Bladder D2cc | — | ≤ 80–85 Gy |
| Rectum D2cc | — | ≤ 65–75 Gy |
| Sigmoid D2cc | — | ≤ 65–75 Gy |

## ABS-Recommended Fractions for HDR

| Regimen | Dose | Fractions |
|---------|------|-----------|
| 4 × 6 Gy | 24 Gy | 4 fx |
| 5 × 5.5 Gy | 27.5 Gy | 5 fx |
| 5 × 6 Gy | 30 Gy | 5 fx |

(All with EBRT 45 Gy; for cumulative EQD2 calculation.)

## Key Recommendations
- MRI-based planning is preferred; CT is acceptable if MRI contraindicated
- HR-CTV D90 ≥ 85 Gy EQD2 is the standard
- IC/IS applicator recommended for bulky (> 4 cm) or asymmetric disease
- Outpatient delivery appropriate for HDR; interstitial or intracavitary placement by qualified practitioners
