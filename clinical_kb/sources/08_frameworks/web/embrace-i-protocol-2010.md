# EMBRACE — An International Study on MRI-Guided Brachytherapy in Locally Advanced Cervical Cancer

**Source URL:** https://www.embracestudy.dk
**Study Registration:** ClinicalTrials.gov NCT00920920
**Coordinating Centre:** Aarhus University Hospital, Denmark
**Start Year:** 2008
**Enrollment:** 1416 patients (closed 2015)
**Principal Investigator:** Prof. Kari Tanderup, Prof. Richard Pötter (Medical University of Vienna)
**Fetched:** 2026-06-17

---

## Document Type
International prospective observational cohort study (GEC-ESTRO / ESTRO endorsed).

## Primary Objective
- Document outcomes of MRI-guided adaptive brachytherapy (IGABT) in locally advanced cervix cancer
- Establish robust dose-response / dose-volume relationships for HR-CTV, IR-CTV, and OARs

## Treatment Protocol
- EBRT: 45–50 Gy in 1.8 Gy fractions, with concomitant cisplatin (40 mg/m² weekly)
- BT: 4 fractions × 6–9 Gy HDR or equivalent PDR, started after external beam
- Applicator: tandem + ovoids/rings; combined intracavitary/interstitial (IC/IS) allowed for large/irregular tumors
- Image-guided adaptive planning per GEC-ESTRO / ICRU 89

## Key Dose Goals
| Structure | Goal |
|-----------|------|
| HR-CTV D90 | ≥ 85 Gy EQD2 (α/β=10) — aim 90 Gy |
| IR-CTV D90 | ≥ 60 Gy EQD2 (α/β=10) |
| Bladder D2cc | ≤ 80–90 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) |
| Rectum D2cc | ≤ 70–75 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) |
| Sigmoid D2cc | ≤ 70–75 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3) |

## Key Results (Published)
- 5-year local control: 92% (stage IIB), 86% (stage IIIB), 74% (stage IVA)
- HR-CTV D90 strongly correlated with local control (dose-response)
- OAR D2cc strongly correlated with late toxicity
- Reduced late toxicity vs. historical point-A series
