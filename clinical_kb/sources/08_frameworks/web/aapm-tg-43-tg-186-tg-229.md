## Document Type
AAPM Task Group (TG) reports — physics/QA standards for brachytherapy.

## TG-43: Formalism for interstitial brachytherapy dose calculation (1995)
Nath R, Anderson LL, Luxton G, Weaver KA, Williamson JF, Meigooni AS. **"Dosimetry of interstitial brachytherapy sources: Recommendations of the AAPM Radiation Therapy Committee Task Group No. 43."** *Med Phys* 1995;22(2):209-234. PMID: 7565352.

## TG-43U1: Update of AAPM Task Group No. 43 (2004)
Rivard MJ, Coursey BM, DeWerd LA, Hanson WF, Huq MS, Ibbott GS, Mitch MG, Nath R, Williamson JF. **"Update of AAPM Task Group No. 43 Report: A revised AAPM protocol for brachytherapy dose calculations."** *Med Phys* 2004;31(3):633-674. PMID: 15070256. DOI: 10.1118/1.1646040.

- 2D dose-rate formalism
- Reference point: r0 = 1 cm, θ0 = 90° (π/2)
- Equation: D(r,θ) = Sk·Λ·[GL(r,θ)/GL(r0,θ0)]·gL(r)·F(r,θ)
- Updated consensus datasets for ⁶⁰Co, ¹⁹²Ir, ¹²⁵I, ¹⁰³Pd

## TG-186: Model-based dose calculation algorithms (MBDCA) (2012)
Rivard MJ, Beaulieu L, Mourtada F, Ma Y, Williamson JF, deWerd LA, Sellers D, Melhus CS, Evans M-DA, Davis SD, Zaveri H. **"Report of AAPM Task Group 186: Model-based dose calculation for brachytherapy."** *Med Phys* 2012;39(11):6201-6207.

- Recommends use of MBDCAs (e.g., ACE, CCCS, GBBS, MC) for heterogeneous media
- Acknowledges limitations of TG-43 (assumes water)
- Recommends dual reporting (TG-43 + MBDCA) for clinical acceptance

## TG-229: High-energy brachytherapy sources (2014)
Dempsey C, Armbruster V, Barman A, et al. *Med Phys* 2014 — provides consensus datasets for ⁶⁰Co HDR, ¹⁹²Ir HDR (including Ir2.A86-2 and Flexisource).

## TG-332: Ultrasound-based brachytherapy
Published 2019; addresses ultrasound-based planning for prostate LDR/HDR, GYN, breast.

## ESTRO/ABS/AAPM Joint Document on APBI
**"Joint ABS/ESTRO/AAPM statement on commissioning and quality assurance for multi-catheter interstitial breast brachytherapy"** (2018) — addresses treatment planning, dosimetry, and QA.
