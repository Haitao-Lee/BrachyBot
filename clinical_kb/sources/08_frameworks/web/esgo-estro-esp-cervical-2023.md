## Brachytherapy Recommendations

### Standard Treatment for LACC
- Concomitant chemoradiotherapy (cisplatin 40 mg/m² weekly) + brachytherapy is the **standard of care**
- **Image-guided adaptive brachytherapy (IGABT)** is **mandatory**
- HR-CTV D90 target: **≥ 85 Gy EQD2 (α/β=10)**; aim 90 Gy when OAR constraints allow
- IC/IS (intracavitary + interstitial) applicator recommended for HR-CTV > 4 cm or poor geometry

### Doses and Constraints
| Parameter | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| HR-CTV D90 (EQD2) | ≥ 85 Gy, aim 90 Gy |
| IR-CTV D90 (EQD2) | ≥ 60 Gy |
| Bladder D2cc (EQD2) | ≤ 80 Gy (α/β=3) |
| Rectum D2cc (EQD2) | ≤ 70 Gy (α/β=3) |
| Sigmoid D2cc (EQD2) | ≤ 70 Gy (α/β=3) |
| Bowel D2cc (EQD2) | ≤ 70 Gy (α/β=3) |

### Trachelectomy / Adjuvant
- For early-stage cervical cancer patients desiring fertility preservation: radical trachelectomy or neoadjuvant chemotherapy considered; BT may be used in selected small-volume disease

## Significance
- Reaffirms IGABT as standard of care in Europe
- Aligns with EMBRACE-II dose goals (90 Gy EQD2 HR-CTV D90)
- Drives updates to NCCN and ESMO
