**Publisher:** National Comprehensive Cancer Network (NCCN)

**Year:** 2024

**URL:** https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1419

## Summary

NCCN guideline for cancer management. The brachytherapy section covers:

- Patient selection criteria for brachytherapy
- Applicator types and techniques
- Dose prescriptions and fractionation schedules
- Integration with external beam radiotherapy
- Follow-up and surveillance recommendations
