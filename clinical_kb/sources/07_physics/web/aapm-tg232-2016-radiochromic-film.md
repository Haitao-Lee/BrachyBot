## Common Films

- **GAFChromic EBT** (EBT1, EBT2, EBT3, EBT4, EBT-XD) — 0.1–40 Gy, ~12 bit dynamic range, 1 cGy sensitivity
- **GAFChromic HD-V2** — 10–1000 Gy
- **GAFChromic MD-55 / MD-V3** — 3–100 Gy

## Key Dosimetry Recommendations

1. **Scanner characterization**: red-green-blue channel correction, flatbed uniformity correction, warm-up ≥ 30 min
2. **Calibration curve**: fitted to functional form such as netOD vs log(D) or rational function; polynomial of order 3-5 typical
3. **Resolution**: 75–300 dpi, optical density < 1.4 to avoid saturation nonlinearity
4. **Film orientation**: portrait/landscape consistent; for EBT3, mark orientation with notched corner
5. **Handling**: gloves only, store at < 25°C and < 70% humidity
6. **Post-irradiation development**: ≥ 24 h for EBT3, longer for EBT-XD (~6 h)
7. **Energy dependence**: small (<5%) for EBT3 in 50 kVp to 6 MV range, but spectral differences for low-energy BT sources (¹²⁵I, ⁵⁰keV ¹⁶⁹Yb) require explicit calibration

## Application to BT QA

- 2D dose distribution verification for single-dwell and multi-dwell HDR
- Source position verification via film autoradiography
- End-to-end QA with phantom

## Typical Uncertainty

- 1D profile: ~2% (k=1) when protocol is well-controlled
- 2D map: 2–3% (k=1)
- Position resolution: ~0.5 mm
