# ICRU Report 58: Dose and Volume Specification for Interstitial Therapy

**Publisher:** ICRU, 1997

**URL:** https://www.icru.org/home/reports/dose-and-volume-specification-for-reporting-interstitial-therapy-report-58

---

## Key Concepts

- **Minimum Target Dose (MTD)**: Minimum dose within the target volume
- **Reference Volume**: Volume enclosed by a specified isodose surface
- **Dose Non-uniformity Ratio (DNR)**: Ratio of volumes receiving high vs reference dose
- Central plane dose reporting
- Dose-rate recommendations for pulsed dose-rate (PDR) and HDR

## Notes for downstream agent

Interstitial BT dose reporting standard. Complements ICRU 38 (intracavitary) and ICRU 89 (cervix).
