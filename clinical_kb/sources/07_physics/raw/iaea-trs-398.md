# IAEA TRS-398: Absorbed Dose Determination

**Publisher:** International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), 2000

**URL:** https://www.iaea.org/publications/6848/absorbed-dose-determination-in-external-beam-radiotherapy

---

## Relevance to Brachytherapy

TRS-398 provides the protocol for absorbed dose determination in photon and electron beams. The brachytherapy section covers:

- Source strength calibration (air kerma rate, reference air kerma rate)
- Dose calculation using TG-43 formalism
- Calibration laboratory requirements
- Traceability to primary standards

## Notes for downstream agent

International reference protocol for dose calibration. Used alongside AAPM TG-43 for brachytherapy source calibration.
