# BRACHY Trial — EBRT +/- HDRIB for advanced NSCLC

## Source
- PMID: 36610615
- Journal: IJROBP 2023 Jul 1;116(3):601-610
- Multicenter Canadian RCT

## Methods
- 134 patients (planned 250), closed early for slow accrual
- Symptomatic stage III/IV NSCLC with endobronchial disease
- Randomized: EBRT alone (20 Gy/5 fx or 30 Gy/10 fx) vs EBRT + HDRIB (14 Gy/2 fx, 1 week apart)
- Mean age 69.8; 67% metastatic
- Primary endpoint: symptom improvement at 6 weeks on Lung Cancer Symptom Scale (LCSS)

## Outcomes
- 6-week symptom improvement: 28.4% EBRT vs 29.9% EBRT+HDRIB (p=0.84)
- Per-protocol: 40.4% vs 47.6% (p=0.49)
- Hemoptysis significantly improved with HDRIB (p=0.03)
- No difference in PFS or OS
- Grade 3/4 toxicities similar

## Key takeaways
- Adding HDRIB to EBRT did not significantly improve overall symptom scores
- Hemoptysis specifically benefited
- Supports selective use of HDRIB for hemoptysis-dominant disease
- Largest randomized dataset for endobronchial BT
