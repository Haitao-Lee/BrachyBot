## Brain brachytherapy
- GliaSite (I-125 balloon) and Cs-131 collagen tiles (GammaTile) are the dominant intra-cavitary brain BT modalities
- IOHDR brain BT (intraoperative HDR with catheters) used in some centers
- Recurrent glioma and selected newly diagnosed GBM candidates

## Pediatric brachytherapy
- Rhabdomyosarcoma, soft tissue sarcoma, genitourinary
- Highly specialized; requires experienced team
- Often LDR with Ir-192 or HDR with IOHDR
- Multimodal approach (chemo + RT)
- Limited centers

## Lung brachytherapy
- HDREB (HDR endobronchial brachytherapy)
- 10 Gy x 1 fraction common; 7.5 Gy x 3; 14 Gy x 2 weekly
- Combined with EBRT for stage III/IV NSCLC
- Palliative hemoptysis, dyspnea, post-obstructive atelectasis
