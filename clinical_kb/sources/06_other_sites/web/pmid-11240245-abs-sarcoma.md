# ABS Guidelines for Soft Tissue Sarcoma Brachytherapy (2001)

## Source
- PMID: 11240245
- IJROBP 2001 Mar 15;49(4):1033-43
- ABS expert panel; Ohio State University lead

## Indications
- Brachytherapy alone or combined with EBRT is established for adjuvant local treatment after resection of STS in adults and children
- Typical scenario: close/positive margins, high-grade extremity sarcoma

## Brachytherapy options
- LDR with Ir-192 or I-125
- Fractionated HDR
- Intraoperative HDR (IOHDR)
- Dose rates and prescription depend on technique

## Key technical points
- Catheter placement intraoperative in tumor bed
- Afterloading after wound healing (typically 5-7 days post-op)
- Dose: LDR 45-50 Gy over 4-6 days
- HDR fractionated equivalent regimens documented
- Combined with EBRT 45-50 Gy + BT boost 15-25 Gy

## Role
- BT enhances therapeutic ratio (biologic + dosimetric advantage)
- Allows higher local dose with sparing of surrounding normal tissue
- Reduced integral dose vs EBRT boosts

- Pre-dates 2023 ABS sarcoma update; see more recent consensus for current recommendations
