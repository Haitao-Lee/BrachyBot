# AAPM TG-129 Notched COMS Plaque — Optic Disc Sparing

## Source
- PMID: 32656945
- Journal of Applied Clinical Medical Physics 2020 Sep;21(9):57-70
- University of Arizona

## Methods
- AAPM TG-43U1 dosimetry formalism with line source approximation
- Implemented in MATLAB using COMS seed coordinates from AAPM TG-129
- Compared standard vs notched COMS plaques
- Notched variants: Case 1 (one seed removed) 12-22 mm; Case 2 (two seeds removed) 14-22 mm
- Standard prescription: 85 Gy in 168 h (7 days) to prescription depth

## Dose prescription
- 85 Gy at prescription depth over 168 h (7 days) is the COMS standard
- Prescription depth typically = tumor apex height
- Plaques 12-22 mm diameter in 2 mm increments

## Key results
- Notched plaques reduce optic disc dose
- Optic disc dose reduction increases with increasing prescription depth
- Five unique trend patterns identified
- Useful for peri-papillary tumors to spare optic nerve

## Key takeaways
- AAPM TG-129 is the authoritative dosimetry protocol for ocular plaque BT
- Provides seed coordinates, dose calculation formalism, and quality assurance
- Notched plaques (with deliberate seed removal) used when tumor abuts optic disc
- COMS protocol: 85 Gy to tumor apex over 5-7 days
