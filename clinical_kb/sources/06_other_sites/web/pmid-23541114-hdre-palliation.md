# HDREB 7.5 Gy x 3 fraction regimen for malignant airway obstruction

## Source
- PMID: 23541114
- Journal: Brachytherapy 2013 Sep-Oct;12(5):471-478
- Hospital das Clínicas, University of São Paulo

## Methods
- 78 patients, prospective observational
- HDREB: 3 fractions of 7.5 Gy at weekly or fortnightly intervals
- +/- external beam irradiation
- Mostly lung cancer primaries; trachea/main bronchi
- Survival follow-up: 3 years

## Outcomes
- 70% overall improvement (Karnofsky / performance status)
- 73.4% improvement in bronchial obstruction
- Confirms 3-fraction regimen (7.5 Gy x 3 = 22.5 Gy total) as an effective palliative schedule

## Key takeaways
- Multiple fractionation schedules are clinically acceptable: 10 Gy x 1, 7.5 Gy x 3, 14 Gy x 2
- All deliver comparable palliation with similar toxicity
- Choice depends on patient logistics, life expectancy, and prior EBRT
