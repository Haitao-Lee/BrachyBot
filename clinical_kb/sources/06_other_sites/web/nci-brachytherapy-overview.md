# NCI — Brachytherapy to Treat Cancer (Patient Reference)

## Source
- National Cancer Institute overview page
- Authoritative US government source

## Indications mentioned
- Breast (APBI, boost)
- Cervical
- Prostate
- Head and neck
- Eye (ocular melanoma)
- Skin
- Brain
- Lung (endobronchial)
- Esophageal
- Bile duct
- Soft tissue sarcoma
- Rectal
- Vaginal / vulvar

## Technical descriptions
- Internal radiation placed inside or next to tumor
- Temporary (HDR afterloading) or permanent (seeds)
- Used alone or with EBRT

## Relevance to "other sites" category
- Confirms NCI lists lung, brain, eye, soft tissue sarcoma, and rare sites as established BT indications
- Establishes brachytherapy as a mainstream modality beyond the typical GYN/prostate/breast/h&n sites
