# 106Ru Plaque Brachytherapy for Uveal Melanoma — Essen Protocol

## Source
- PMID: 30320093
- Ocular Oncology and Pathology 2018 Sep;4(2):122-128
- University Hospital Essen, TU Dortmund

## Background
- 106Ru plaques are the most common European treatment for small-to-medium uveal melanoma
- 106Ru is a beta emitter (3.54 MeV max) with limited range
- Prescription conventions differ by institution (no universal standard)

## Essen dose prescription
- 700 Gy minimum to tumor base
- 130 Gy minimum to tumor apex
- Contrast with COMS 85 Gy to apex for I-125

## Methods
- Monte Carlo dose simulations on 3 plaque types
- Assessed 85 Gy isodose shape and treatment margins
- Apical margin: at least 0.8 mm expansion beyond tumor apex for all plaques
- Lateral margin: extends beyond visible plaque edge for symmetric plaques

## Key takeaways
- Two distinct prescription philosophies exist:
  1. COMS: 85 Gy to tumor apex (US, I-125)
  2. Essen: 130 Gy apex + 700 Gy base (Europe, 106Ru)
- Different dose metrics, different biologic effects
- 85 Gy apex for I-125 is well-established with strong clinical data
