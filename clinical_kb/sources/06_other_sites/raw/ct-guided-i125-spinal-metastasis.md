# CT-guided I-125 seed implantation for spinal metastatic tumors

**Source:** OncoTargets and Therapy 2016;9:7-12
**DOI:** 10.2147/OTT.S95410

## Abstract

**Objective:** To examine safety and clinical efficacy of CT-guided I-125 seed implantation for spinal metastatic tumors.

**Methods:** 20 cases of spinal metastatic tumors, median 19 seeds implanted, MPD 90-130 Gy.

**Results:**
- Pain relief rate: 95%
- Median control time: 12.5 months
- 3-, 6-, 12-month local control rates: 100%, 95%, 60%
- Median survival: 16 months
- No major complications, no seed migration

**Conclusion:** CT-guided I-125 seed implantation is safe and effective for spinal metastases.

## Notes for downstream agent

Demonstrates I-125 seed brachytherapy efficacy for bone metastases. Relevant for palliative brachytherapy applications.
