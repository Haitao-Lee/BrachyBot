# CT-guided I-125 seed implantation for early lung cancer

**Source:** J Thorac Dis 2020;12(10):5996-6009
**DOI:** 10.21037/jtd-20-2666

## Abstract

**Background:** To analyze the efficacy of CT-guided implantation of 125I radioactive particles in treatment of early lung cancer.

**Methods:** Six patients with early lung cancer (stage I-II), including 4 squamous cell carcinoma, 1 adenocarcinoma, and 1 small cell lung cancer. TPS software used for dose calculation. 20-55 particles implanted per site, particle spacing 0.5-1.0 cm.

**Results:**
- 1 month: 2 CR, 4 PR
- 6 months: 3 CR, 2 PR
- 12 months: 3 CR, 1 PR, 1 PD
- 24 months: All CR
- No serious complications

**Conclusions:** 125I radioactive particle implantation is safe, reliable and effective for early lung cancer.

## Notes for downstream agent

Demonstrates I-125 seed brachytherapy for early-stage lung cancer in patients who cannot undergo surgery.
