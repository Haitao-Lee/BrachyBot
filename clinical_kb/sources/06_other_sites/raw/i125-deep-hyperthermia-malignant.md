# I-125 seed implantation with deep hyperthermia for malignant tumors

**Source:** J Contemp Brachytherapy 2024;16(3):202-210
**DOI:** 10.5114/jcb.2024.141407

## Abstract

**Purpose:** To analyze clinical efficacy of I-125 radioactive particle implantation with deep hyperthermia in malignant tumor treatment.

**Methods:** 60 patients with malignant tumors, CT-guided I-125 implantation followed by deep hyperthermia for 3 days post-surgery.

**Results:**
- ORR: 73.33%
- DCR: 81.67%
- Pain relief: 81.67%
- Improved quality of life
- No adverse reactions ≥ level 2

**Conclusions:** I-125 implantation with deep hyperthermia is effective combination therapy for malignant tumors.

## Notes for downstream agent

Demonstrates combined I-125 brachytherapy + hyperthermia approach for various malignant tumors.
