## Topics Covered

### LDR Permanent Seed Implant
- I-125 144 Gy monotherapy / 110 Gy boost
- Pd-103 125 Gy monotherapy / 90-100 Gy boost
- Cs-131 115 Gy monotherapy / 85 Gy boost
- Day 30 CT post-implant dosimetry
- Real-time TRUS planning

### HDR Afterloading (Ir-192)
- Single-fraction 19 Gy
- Two-fraction 13.5-15 Gy
- Multi-fraction monotherapy (38/4, 34.5/3, 27/2)
- EBRT + HDR boost combinations

### Genitourinary Sites
- Prostate: primary, focal, salvage
- Bladder: multicatheter, intravesical
- Penile: interstitial HDR
- Urethra: as OAR in prostate BT

### Dose Metrics
- D90, V100, V150, V200 (target)
- D10, D30 (urethra)
- D2cc, D0.1cc (rectum, bladder)
- mPD, mRD (TG-43)
