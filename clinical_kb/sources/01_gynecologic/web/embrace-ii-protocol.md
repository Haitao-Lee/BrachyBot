## Protocol Aims (planning aims)
Tighter constraints than EMBRACE-I:

| Structure | EMBRACE-II aim (EQD2) |
|---|---|
| HR-CTV D90 (α/β=10) | ≥ 90 Gy (recommended 90–95 Gy) |
| IR-CTV D90 (α/β=10) | ≥ 60 Gy |
| Bladder D2cc (α/β=3) | ≤ 80 Gy (aim 75 Gy) |
| Rectum D2cc (α/β=3) | ≤ 65 Gy (aim 60 Gy) |
| Sigmoid D2cc (α/β=3) | ≤ 65 Gy (aim 60 Gy) |
| Bowel D2cc (α/β=3) | ≤ 65 Gy (aim 60 Gy) |

## EBRT specifications
- 45–50 Gy in 25 fractions, IG-IMRT, simultaneous integrated boost (SIB) to involved nodes to 55–60 Gy in 25 fractions.
- ITV (nodal) = CTV + 5–7 mm margin; PTV = ITV + 5 mm.
- Parametrial boost: 55–60 Gy to parametrium if extensive parametrial disease.

## BT specifications
- 4 × 7 Gy HR-CTV D90 OR 3 × 8.5 Gy OR 4 × 6.5 Gy (institutional variation within protocol).
- Pulse: 1 pulse/hour; total treatment time 25–30 working days (EBRT + BT combined).
- MRI-based planning for at least 1st fraction; CT acceptable for subsequent fractions.

## Systemic therapy
- Cisplatin 40 mg/m² weekly × 5 cycles (concurrent with EBRT).
- Optional: induction chemotherapy (still experimental in EMBRACE-II).

## Initial results (2024 update)
- 5-year local control: 95% (vs 92% in EMBRACE-I), particularly stage IIIC1 nodal cohort.
- Reduced grade ≥3 morbidity: rectal 6%, bladder 4%, vaginal stenosis 13%.
- Confirms dose-painting escalation strategy for HR-CTV while pushing OAR sparing.

## Sample size / accrual
- Target n=1000.
- Actual enrollment complete: 960 evaluable (as of 2024).
