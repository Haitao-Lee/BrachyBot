## Scope
- Definitive/adjuvant brachytherapy for endometrial cancer (post-hysterectomy).
- Covers both VCB alone (intermediate-risk) and VCB boost (post-EBRT).

## Indication Summary
| Risk group | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Low-risk (stage IA, grade 1–2, no LVSI) | Observation (no VCB) |
| Intermediate-risk (PORTEC-2 / GOG 99 criteria) | VCB alone |
| High-intermediate-risk | VCB alone OR EBRT + VCB (institutional) |
| High-risk (stage III, serous, clear cell) | ChemoRT (EBRT + VCB) ± chemo |
| Stage I serous / clear cell | Consider EBRT + VCB (VBT alone insufficient) |

## VCB Dose (Image-Guided 3D)
- **HR-CTV:** upper 3 cm of vagina; full cylinder of 5 cm for high-risk.
- **Dose to HR-CTV D90:** EQD2 (α/β=10) ≈ 60–70 Gy for VCB alone.
- **Surface dose:** aim 80–100 Gy EQD2 (α/β=3).

### Common HDR Schedules
| Schedule | EQD2 (α/β=3) to 5 mm depth | EQD2 (α/β=10) |
|---|---|---|
| 6 × 5 Gy | 37.5 Gy | 32.5 Gy |
| 3 × 7 Gy | 32.2 Gy | 25.2 Gy |
| 5 × 4.5 Gy | 28.1 Gy | 22.0 Gy |
| 4 × 6 Gy | 28.0 Gy | 22.7 Gy |

## Applicator
- **Multichannel cylinder** strongly preferred (spares anterior rectal wall).
- Single-channel cylinder acceptable.
- 25–35 mm diameter (largest comfortable).

## OAR Constraints (cumulative EQD2, α/β=3, VCB alone)
| OAR | Aim (Gy EQD2) |
|---|---|
| Bladder D2cc | ≤ 80 |
| Rectum D2cc | ≤ 60–65 |
| Sigmoid D2cc | ≤ 65 |
| Bowel D2cc | ≤ 65 |
| Vaginal mucosa (surface) | 80–120 |

## Image Guidance
- CT-based planning (MRI optional).
- Delineate: HR-CTV (upper 3 cm vagina), bladder, rectum, sigmoid, bowel.
- Cylinder reconstruction on planning CT (marker wire in lumen).
- 3 mm slice thickness.

## 2024 Updates vs 2012 GEC-ESTRO
- Multichannel cylinder is now the recommended default (was alternative).
- HR-CTV = upper 3 cm is now standard (was upper 4–5 cm).
- 6 × 5 Gy at 5 mm depth is the standard dose.
- Quality-of-life emphasis: minimize vaginal stenosis with dilator use.
- Lower dose schedules (3 × 7 Gy) still acceptable; choice based on institutional protocol.

## Special Considerations
- **Serous, clear cell, carcinosarcoma histology:** more aggressive; VCB alone may be insufficient.
- **Lymphovascular space invasion (LVSI):** add EBRT if extensive.
- **Recurrent EC at vaginal cuff:** Re-irradiation with BT (interstitial) is possible; cumulative vaginal dose ≤ 150 Gy EQD2.
