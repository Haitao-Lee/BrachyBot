# Postoperative Vaginal Cuff Brachytherapy (VCB) for Endometrial Cancer: PORTEC-3 Context

## PORTEC-2 (Vaginal Cuff BT vs EBRT for Intermediate-Risk)
- **Population:** 427 patients with intermediate-risk endometrial cancer.
- **Design:** Non-inferiority, randomized to VCB vs EBRT (46 Gy).
- **Result:** VCB alone is non-inferior to EBRT for vaginal control (98% vs 97% at 5 years).
- **Toxicity:** VCB significantly less GI toxicity; less impact on quality of life.
- **Conclusion:** VCB is the standard of care for intermediate-risk endometrial cancer.

## PORTEC-3 (Chemoradiation for High-Risk)
- **Population:** 660 patients with high-risk endometrial cancer (stage I grade 3 with deep myometrial invasion or LVSI, stage II-III).
- **Design:** Randomized to chemoradiation (cisplatin + EBRT + carboplatin/paclitaxel × 4) vs EBRT alone.
- **Result:** 5-year OS 81.4% (chemoRT) vs 76.1% (RT) — HR 0.79.
- **VBT component:** Most patients received VCB boost after EBRT.
- **Conclusion:** ChemoRT is standard of care for high-risk EC; VCB adds local control.

## VCB Dose (Modern Standard)
- **3D image-based VCB:** 6 × 5 Gy prescribed to 5 mm depth from applicator surface.
- **Alternative:** 3 × 7 Gy to 5 mm depth.
- **Lower-dose protocols:** 5 × 4.5 Gy, 3 × 10 Gy (in 2 fractions/week), 4 × 6 Gy.
- **Aim:** EQD2 (α/β=3) to vaginal mucosa = 60–80 Gy.

## Applicator Selection
- **Single-channel cylinder (28–35 mm):** most common, dose symmetric.
- **Multichannel cylinder:** allows asymmetric loading; reduces dose to anterior rectal wall and posterior bladder neck.
- **Ovoid (rare):** only if vaginal anatomy allows.

## Treatment Volume
- **HR-CTV:** upper 3 cm of vagina (most recurrences here).
- **Full-length cylinder (5 cm treatment length):** for stage II or risk of distal recurrence.

## 2024 GEC-ESTRO/ESTRO Consensus Update on VCB
- Image-guided VCB strongly recommended (CT or MRI).
- Multichannel cylinder preferred when available (spares anterior rectal wall).
- For serous or clear cell histology: VCB alone is insufficient; consider EBRT + VCB.
- VCB alone: 6 × 5 Gy to 5 mm depth (or institutional equivalent).
- 3-year vaginal control: 98%+.
