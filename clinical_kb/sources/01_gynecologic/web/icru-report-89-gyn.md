## Goals
- Standardize 3D image-guided brachytherapy (IGBT) terminology, contouring, dose reporting.
- Replace ICRU 38 (point-A based) system.
- Provide unified dose metrics: D90, D2cc, D0.1cc, V100, V150, V200.

## Required Targets
1. **GTV (BT)** — visible tumor at time of BT.
2. **HR-CTV** — high-risk CTV (GTV + residual disease + entire cervix + parametrial invasion + adjacent uterine body).
3. **IR-CTV** — intermediate-risk CTV (initial disease extent at diagnosis + 5–15 mm margin, capped at anatomic boundaries).

## Required OARs
- **Bladder:** entire outer wall, inner surface excluded; D2cc reported.
- **Rectum:** mucosa excluded; D2cc reported.
- **Sigmoid:** full thickness; D2cc reported.
- **Bowel (small bowel loops):** peritonealized segments; D2cc reported.
- **Vagina (PIBS, PIBS ± 2 cm):** lateral vaginal walls at PIBS (posterior inferior border of symphysis) and 2 cm above.

## Required Dose Metrics
| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| D90 | minimum dose to 90% of target volume |
| D98 | minimum dose to 98% of target volume |
| D2cc | minimum dose to 2 cc of OAR |
| D0.1cc | minimum dose to 0.1 cc of OAR (hot spot) |
| V100 | target volume receiving ≥ 100% of prescribed dose |
| V150 | target volume receiving ≥ 150% of prescribed dose |
| V200 | target volume receiving ≥ 200% of prescribed dose |

## Dose Reporting Standard
- **Total dose (EBRT + BT)** reported as **EQD2** (equivalent dose in 2 Gy fractions).
- Tumor EQD2 uses α/β=10; OAR EQD2 uses α/β=3.
- Linear-quadratic (LQ) model assumed; gaps in treatment should be compensated (if > 1 day).
- Pulse structure: report number of pulses, total time, dose per pulse.

## Prescribed Doses (typical)
- HR-CTV D90 cumulative EQD2: 85–90 Gy
- IR-CTV D90 cumulative EQD2: 60–65 Gy
- Bladder D2cc: ≤ 90 Gy EQD2 (aim 80)
- Rectum D2cc: ≤ 75 Gy EQD2 (aim 65)
- Sigmoid D2cc: ≤ 75 Gy EQD2 (aim 65)
- Bowel D2cc: ≤ 75 Gy EQD2 (aim 65)

## Applicator Reporting
- Each applicator should be uniquely identified, dwell positions pre-calculated, and reconstruction validated with imaging.
- Reconstruction: source path digitization in applicator, with ±2 mm accuracy on CT/MRI.
- Imaging: at least orthogonal radiographs OR CT/MRI for all fractions.
