# Chinese CSTRO/CSCO Guidelines for I-125 Seed Implantation in Pancreatic Cancer

- **URL (CSCO guideline)**: http://www.csco.org.cn/
- **URL (CSTRO consensus)**: https://www.cstro.org/
- **Title**: Chinese Expert Consensus on Iodine-125 Seed Implantation for Pancreatic Cancer (放射性碘125粒子植入治疗胰腺癌专家共识)
- **Authors**: CSTRO Brachytherapy Committee, China Society for Therapeutic Radiology and Oncology
- **Year**: 2017, updated 2020
- **Source type**: National expert consensus

## Key Recommendations

### Indications
- Locally advanced unresectable pancreatic adenocarcinoma (LAPC)
- Postoperative recurrent pancreatic cancer
- Pain palliation from tumor invasion
- Combined with EBRT/chemotherapy
- Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group (ECOG) performance status 0-2

### Contraindications
- Diffuse distant metastases (relative)
- Severe main pancreatic duct obstruction
- Tumor invading GI lumen with impending fistula
- Coagulopathy (INR > 1.5, platelets < 50,000)
- Tumor > 7-8 cm (relative — staged implantation considered)

### Pre-Planning
- Contrast-enhanced CT (3 mm slice thickness)
- GTV = visible tumor on contrast CT
- CTV = GTV + 0.5-1.0 cm margin (modified by anatomy)
- PTV = CTV + 0.5 cm (varies by implant technique)
- Total activity calculation: matched peripheral dose (MPD)
- DVH evaluation: D90, V100, V150, V200

### Activity and Spacing
- Seed activity: 0.4-0.7 mCi per seed (typical 0.5-0.6 mCi)
- Spacing: 0.5-1.0 cm between seeds (center-to-center)
- Seeds per cm³: 1-3 (varies by tumor volume and required MPD)
- MPD: 110-160 Gy (typical prescription: D90 110-140 Gy)
- Activity = tumor volume (cm³) x 5-7 (rough estimate)

### Implantation Techniques
- CT-guided percutaneous transperitoneal approach
- EUS-guided (endoscopic ultrasound) for body/tail tumors
- Intraoperative implantation during surgical exploration
- Applicator: 18-Gauge interstitial needles

### Post-Implant Dosimetry
- CT 24-48 hours post-implant for dosimetric verification
- Acceptable D90 > matched peripheral dose
- V100 > 90% ideal
- Avoid high-dose spillage: V200 < 50% typical

### OAR Constraints
- Duodenum D2cc < 50-60 Gy EQD2
- Stomach D2cc < 50-60 Gy EQD2
- Spinal cord Dmax < 45 Gy
- Liver V30 < 30% (cumulative)

### Clinical Outcomes
- Pain relief: 70-85% (within 1-2 weeks)
- Local control 1-year: 60-80% in LAPC
- Median survival: 10-16 months with combined modality
- Median survival post I-125 alone: 6-10 months

### Adverse Events
- Pancreatitis (5-10%)
- GI bleeding/ulcer (5-10%)
- Pancreatic fistula (< 5%)
- Seed migration to liver (rare, < 1%)

### Key Findings
- 5-year Chinese experience with 1000+ cases published
- Combination with EBRT (30-45 Gy) + chemotherapy shows best outcomes
- Image-guided technique reduces complications
- Post-implant CT dosimetry essential for QA
- Higher activity (0.6-0.7 mCi) with closer spacing (0.5-0.6 cm) common in modern practice
