# 3D-printed coplanar template for I-125 seed implantation in pancreatic cancer

**Source:** World J Gastroenterol 2018;24(46):5280-5287
**DOI:** 10.3748/wjg.v24.i46.5280

## Abstract

**AIM:** To evaluate a 3D-printed coplanar template for iodine-125 seed implantation therapy in patients with pancreatic cancer.

**METHODS:** Retrospective analysis of 25 patients with pancreatic cancer who underwent I-125 seed implantation. Group A (n=12): 3D-printed template guidance. Group B (n=13): free-hand implantation.

**RESULTS:**
- Group A: Pre-operative D90 = 155.32 ± 8.05 Gy, Post-operative D90 = 154.82 ± 16.43 Gy (P > 0.05)
- Group A: V100 = 91.05% ± 4.06%, V150 = 64.54% ± 13.40%
- Group A vs B: V100 = 91.05% vs 72.91% (P < 0.05)
- No major complications

**CONCLUSION:** 3D-printed coplanar template is safe and effective for I-125 seed implantation, improving accuracy and dosimetric distribution.

## Key Findings

- 3D-printed templates significantly improve dosimetric parameters
- V100 (volume receiving 100% of prescription dose) is significantly higher with template guidance
- No major procedure-related complications
- Template enables more consistent needle placement

## Notes for downstream agent

This is a key study demonstrating the clinical value of 3D-printed templates for I-125 seed implantation in pancreatic cancer. Supports the 2023 guideline recommendations.
